    Settings

    Project Settings

    With Cord, you can edit your labelling ontology at any time. Add, delete, or edit labels through the Settings tab in projects.

    You can also delete your project through the Setting tab under the 'Danger Zone' section. Deleting your project does not delete the datasets in the project, but will delete your labels and labelling ontology.
