    Export

    Cord allows you to export your labels with a high degree of granularity. Pick the list of video labels you want exported from the set of videos that are 'In progress' or 'Labelled'. You can mix and match the set of objects and classifications you want to include in your export. Once you have picked the labels you want, click 'Export' and download your labels in an easily parseable json format.
